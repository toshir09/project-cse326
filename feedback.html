Feedback

Positive Experiences

  1. Efficient Online Services: Many users appreciate the department's user-friendly website, making it convenient to file returns and make payments online.
  2. Educational Initiatives: The department's efforts in taxpayer education and outreach have been commended, helping individuals better understand their tax obligations.
  3. Prompt Customer Support: Positive feedback has been received regarding the responsiveness and helpfulness of customer support in addressing queries and concerns.
Areas of Improvement
  1. Simplification of Processes: Some users suggest further simplification of tax processes to make it more accessible for individuals who may find the current procedures complex.
  2. Enhanced Communication: Feedback indicates a desire for clearer and more proactive communication from the department, especially in terms of changes in tax policies and procedures.
  3. Streamlined Grievance Redressal: Users have suggested improvements in the grievance redressal mechanism, emphasizing the need for a more streamlined process for dispute resolution.
General Observations
  1. Modernization Efforts: Users have noted the positive impact of the department's modernization efforts, such as the introduction of e-filing and online payment systems.
  2. Strict Enforcement of Compliance: While some appreciate the strict enforcement measures to curb tax evasion, others express concerns about the intensity of scrutiny and audit processes.
  3. Suggestions for Improvement: Some users have provided constructive suggestions, such as enhanced user guides and FAQs, to assist individuals in navigating the tax filing process more effectively.

Thank you for taking the time to provide us with your valuable feedback!
